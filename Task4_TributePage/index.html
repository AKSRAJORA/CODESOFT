Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). Widely regarded as the “People’s President,” he was known for his humility, scientific brilliance, and commitment to youth development.

His Contributions

  • Developed India’s first indigenous satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Played a crucial role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998
  • Authored inspirational books like *Wings of Fire*
  • Believed in the power of youth and education

Famous Quote

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
